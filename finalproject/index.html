About MuseCen

The Historic Center of Mexico City is home to some of the most remarkable museums in Latin America — all within walking distance of each other.

MuseCen helps locals and tourists discover these cultural spaces, with key details like schedules, prices, and what to expect inside.

From pre-Hispanic artifacts to contemporary art, there is something for every curious visitor.
