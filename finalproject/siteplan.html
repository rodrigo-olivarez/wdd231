SITE PLAN | FINAL PROJECT SITE NAME: MuseCen: This name combine 2 words, "Muse" of Museum and "Cen" of Center, because in the center of Mexico City have many museums around and its a lot, sounds short and friendly. SITE PURPOSE: MuseCen is a three-page informational guide dedicated to the museums of Mexico City Center. The site helps locals and tourists easily explore, and plan visits to these cultural spaces by providing key information such as schedules, admission prices, exhibit types, and locations. SCENARIOS: 1-I love museums but I don't have enough money. Which ones are cheap or free to visit today? 2-My family wants to visit a museum, but there are children in the family. Which museum has a children's area so they can learn too? 3-I'm visiting Mexico City for the first time and I'd like to go to a museum. What are the opening hours of the ones closest to me?
